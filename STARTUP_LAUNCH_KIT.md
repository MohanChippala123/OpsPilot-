# OpsPilot Startup Launch Kit

## One-Liner

OpsPilot is an installable AI front desk for service businesses that turns customer messages into replies, appointments, reminders, and jobs.

## Short Pitch

Small service businesses lose hours every week switching between texts, email, calendars, and job notes. OpsPilot gives them one AI-assisted command center for customer intake, reply drafts, scheduling, and follow-up work.

## Instagram Bio

AI front desk for service businesses. Messages, scheduling, reminders, and jobs in one installable app.

## Instagram Launch Caption

I am building OpsPilot: an AI operations assistant for local service businesses.

The goal is simple: help busy teams handle customer messages, draft replies, book appointments, and turn requests into trackable jobs without hiring another front desk person.

The first public preview is live as an installable app. It is early, but the core workflow is there:

- Capture incoming messages
- Draft AI replies
- Create follow-up jobs
- View appointments
- Install it like an app from the browser

I am looking for feedback from HVAC, cleaning, dental, property management, contractors, and other service businesses.
